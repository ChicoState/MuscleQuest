Chest Day Workout!

When starting out start at a lower weight. Underestimate your strenght. If you can comfortably do a full set from 10-15 reps. Increase weight by 5 pounts until you can't do a full set. Once you cant do a full set of 10-15. Lower the weight by 5 pounds and make that your working weight. Once you feel like you are ready or want to challange yourself add either a 2.5lb or a 5lb weight to your exercise. Remember to work out safely, so make sure to warm up first before on moving to your working weight. EX. (5x(10-15)) = 5 sets of 10-15 reps
